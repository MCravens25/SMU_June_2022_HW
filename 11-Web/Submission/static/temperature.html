Max Temperature

This is our only visual that shows any correlations between the Latitude and the temperature. What we did was take the max temperature of a bunch of cities, and then looked at where the latitude was compared to the equator wich stands at a latitude degree of zero. This Visual shows that correlation in the fact you can see our dots, that represent our value markers are kind of clustered together and you can see a bit of a shape starting to form. Our visual proves that as you get closer to the equator your max temperature is likely to increase, and the further you move away from the equator the less your max temperature would be as you move yourself closer to the poles.
